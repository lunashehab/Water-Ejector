2. Description of Service

Speaker Cleaner Pro is a free, browser-based digital utility designed to generate specific audio frequencies (such as 165Hz sine waves and 60Hz square waves) utilizing the standard Web Audio API. The intended purpose of this service is to assist users in physically vibrating their device's built-in speakers to potentially dislodge trapped moisture, liquid, or light dust particles. The tool requires no downloads, installations, or external hardware to function.
