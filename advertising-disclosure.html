ADVERTISING DISCLOSURE
Financial Transparency
Published by Don Odibat | Last Updated: Feb 2026

At Don System's Holding, we believe in keeping essential digital utilities free and accessible to everyone. To maintain the server costs, development hours, and acoustic research required to run Speaker Cleaner Pro, we utilize specific monetization strategies. This disclosure outlines exactly how we fund this platform.

1. Display Advertising (Google AdSense)

Speaker Cleaner Pro is monetized primarily through display advertising provided by Google AdSense. You will notice banner ads and occasional interstitial ads while navigating the site or reading our guides.

  • Dynamic Content: The content of these advertisements is generated dynamically by Google based on your browsing history, location, and interests (if you have opted into personalized advertising via our Consent Management Platform).
  • No Endorsement: The presence of an advertisement on this site does not constitute an endorsement by Don Systems. We do not explicitly choose the products or services shown in the Google AdSense blocks.

Cookie Compliance: We utilize Google's Funding Choices CMP (Consent Management Platform) to ensure full compliance with GDPR, CPRA, and international privacy laws. Users in regulated regions have total control over whether they are served personalized or non-personalized ads. You can revoke or change your consent at any time via the "Privacy & Cookie Settings" link in our footer.

2. Editorial Independence

Our advertising relationships have absolutely zero impact on our technical guidelines, hardware reviews, or acoustic methodology. We maintain strict editorial independence. No advertiser can pay us to alter a diagnostic result, prioritize a specific brand of smartphone, or publish false hardware information.

3. Affiliate Links

Occasionally, within our guides or articles, we may link to physical hardware tools (such as microfiber cloths, silica gel packets, or specific smartphone models) on third-party websites like Amazon.com.

If you click on an affiliate link and make a purchase, Don System's Holding may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you. These links will always be clearly identified as affiliate links within the text.

4. Direct Support (Buy Me A Coffee)

Because we want to keep the core tool 100% free of paywalls or premium subscriptions, we accept direct support from our users. If Speaker Cleaner Pro saved your device from an expensive repair, you have the option to support our development via the "Buy Me A Coffee" platform linked in the site footer. This is entirely optional and greatly appreciated.
