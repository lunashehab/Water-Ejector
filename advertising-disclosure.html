ADVERTISING DISCLOSURE
Transparency & Funding

Transparency is a core value at Don System's Holding. Running high-bandwidth web utilities, maintaining global server infrastructure, and dedicating development time to creating scientifically accurate hardware guides costs money. This Advertising Disclosure explains exactly how we fund Speaker Cleaner Pro while remaining completely free for the end-user.

1. The "No Paywall" Philosophy

We fundamentally disagree with the business model used by many "Water Eject" apps on the iOS App Store and Google Play Store. We will never lock basic acoustic recovery tools behind a $9.99 weekly subscription, nor will we ever force you to input credit card information to save a wet device. Speaker Cleaner Pro is, and will remain, 100% free to use.

2. The Google AdSense Partnership

To keep the platform free, we have partnered with the Google AdSense display network. You will see banner and display advertisements integrated into the layout of our website. Here is how that relationship works:

  • Algorithmic Serving: The advertisements you see are served programmatically by Google based on your browsing history and cookies (unless you have opted out of personalized ads via our Consent Management Platform).
  • No Direct Endorsements: The presence of a specific advertisement on our site does not constitute an endorsement of that product, service, or company by Don Odibat or Don System's Holding.
  • Strict Filtration: We actively use our AdSense publisher controls to block malicious, predatory, or irrelevant ad categories (such as malware downloads or adult content) from appearing on our site.

Separation of Church and State: Advertisers have absolutely zero influence over our editorial content, our acoustic recovery guides, or the engineering of our web tools. We do not alter our technical advice to appease advertisers.

3. Zero Paid Placements or "Sponsored Tools"

We do not accept money from app developers or hardware repair shops to rank, feature, or promote their specific tools in our guides. If we mention a piece of hardware (like the iPhone 15 or the Samsung Galaxy S24), it is purely for technical and educational context.

4. Affiliate Links & Donations

Currently, Speaker Cleaner Pro does not utilize affiliate marketing (such as Amazon Associates links). We do not earn commissions by recommending you buy physical cleaning brushes or phone cases.

The only external financial link on this website is our "Buy Me A Coffee" donation link. This is an entirely optional, third-party platform where users who successfully saved their devices using our free tool can voluntarily choose to tip the developer. These direct donations go straight toward server hosting fees.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Our advertising model relies on standard web cookies. We are fully compliant with GDPR and CPRA laws. You maintain complete control over what advertising data you share. To view, modify, or revoke your consent for personalized advertisements, please click the "Privacy & Cookie Settings" link located at the bottom of any page on this site.
