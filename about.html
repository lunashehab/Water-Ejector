About Don Systems

Audio Frequency Solutions • Tupelo, MS

Commercial Mission

Don Systems is a private digital publishing entity specializing in browser-based audio diagnostics. Founded in 2025, our goal is to democratize access to hardware maintenance tools. We believe that simple moisture ejection shouldn't require expensive trips to repair shops or paid app subscriptions.

Engineering Standards

The "Speaker Cleaner Pro" utility is built on the Web Audio API standard. Unlike generic MP3 files which suffer from compression artifacts, our engine generates pure sinusoidal waves in real-time on your local device. This ensures the 165Hz frequency is mathematically perfect, optimizing safe displacement for modern smartphone diaphragms.
