About Don Systems

Speaker Cleaner Pro is a utility developed by Don Systems, a web technology group based in Tupelo, MS.

Our mission is simple: to save hardware from preventable water damage without the need for expensive repairs or paid applications. We believe that basic hardware maintenance tools should be free, accessible, and privacy-focused.

The Technology

This tool utilizes the Web Audio API to generate specific sine, square, and sawtooth waveforms ranging from 60Hz to 12000Hz.

Through extensive testing on Apple and Android hardware, we identified 165Hz as the optimal resonant frequency for displacing water from modern smartphone speaker chambers. This frequency creates enough air pressure to push water out of the speaker grill mesh without exceeding the safe amplitude limits of the diaphragm.
