About Don Systems

EST. 2025 | Audio Utility Division

Our Mission

At Don Systems, we believe hardware maintenance should be free, accessible, and scientifically accurate. In an era where smartphones are sealed units with non-removable batteries, water damage remains a primary cause of device failure. Our mission is to provide a "First Aid" response tool that is instantly available in any browser, worldwide.

The Technology

The "Speaker Cleaner Pro" utility was built on the Web Audio API standard. Unlike MP3 files which are compressed and lose fidelity, our engine generates pure sinusoidal waves in real-time. This ensures that the 165Hz frequency you hear is mathematically perfect, providing the maximum safe displacement for your speaker diaphragm.

Why We Do It

Repair shops often charge upwards of $50 just to look at a water-damaged phone. By democratizing access to high-frequency ejection tools, we help millions of users extend the lifespan of their devices, reducing electronic waste and saving consumer costs.
