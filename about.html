ABOUT US

Welcome to Speaker Cleaner Pro, a utility created by Don Systems. We specialize in building accessible, non-invasive digital tools designed to extend the lifespan of modern consumer electronics.

Our Mission

In a world where smartphones cost upwards of $1,000, physical hardware degradation is a massive pain point for consumers. The majority of audio issues—muffled sound, low volume, and crackling—are not caused by permanent electrical failure, but by simple physical blockages like water and dust.

Our mission is to provide a safe, free, and instantly accessible alternative to expensive repair shops and dangerous DIY methods (like the infamous "rice trick" or using needles to dig out lint). We believe that the hardware inside your phone is powerful enough to clean itself, provided it is given the correct mathematical instructions.

The Science Behind the Tool

Speaker Cleaner Pro operates on the principles of acoustic engineering and fluid dynamics. By leveraging the HTML5 Web Audio API, our web application generates mathematically pure, uncompressed waveforms directly from your device's audio processor.

  • Sine Waves (165Hz - 440Hz): Engineered to match the mechanical resonance of mobile micro-speakers, creating the massive air pressure needed to break the surface tension of water and forcefully eject it.
  • Square Waves (60Hz - 120Hz): Designed to snap the speaker diaphragm violently back and forth, acting as an acoustic jackhammer to shatter hardened crusts of pocket lint and skin oils.

Privacy First

We built Speaker Cleaner Pro as a web application specifically to avoid the pitfalls of native app stores. You do not need to download an app, grant microphone permissions, or share your location to use our tool. The audio generation happens entirely locally on your device's RAM within the browser sandbox, ensuring 100% privacy and security.
