Speaker Cleaner: The Ultimate Maintenance Guide

Over time, your smartphone speaker becomes a magnet for pocket lint, skin oils, and dust. This "audio crust" gradually muffles your sound until your maximum volume feels like 50%.

The Dangers of Physical Cleaning

NEVER use needles, toothpicks, or compressed air directly into the speaker grill. You risk puncturing the delicate waterproof membrane or pushing dust deeper into the internal components.

Sonic Cleaning: The Professional Choice

Our Speaker Cleaner uses Square Waves and Sawtooth Waves. Unlike the smooth Sine waves used for water, these "sharper" waves create intense micro-vibrations that help shatter hardened dust particles, making them easier to shake out.

Signs You Need a Speaker Clean:

  • Distorted audio at high volumes.
  • Crackling noises during phone calls.
  • The "Earpiece" speaker is significantly quieter than the bottom speaker.

Maintaining Your Device

We recommend running a 30-second cleaning cycle once a month. This prevents the accumulation of grime that requires professional disassembly to fix.
