Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

At WaterInMySpeaker.com ("we", "us", "our"), the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information received and collected by our website and how it is used.

1. No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share your personal data.

This website functions as a "client-side" utility. The audio frequencies generated for cleaning your speakers are created mathematically within your web browser (using the Web Audio API). No audio files are downloaded, and no usage data is sent to our servers.

2. Log Files

Like many other websites, we make use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks. This information is used to analyze trends, administer the site, and track user's movement around the site. IP addresses and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

3. Cookies and Web Beacons

We do not use cookies to track your personal history. However, third-party vendors may use cookies to serve ads.

Google DoubleClick DART Cookie

  • Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site.
  • Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to users based on their visit to our site and other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4. Hardware Disclaimer

This utility generates high-frequency and low-frequency sounds designed to displace water. While these frequencies are generally safe for consumer electronics, you use this tool at your own risk. We are not responsible for any hardware damage that may occur from the use of this website or from the original water damage itself.

5. Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our privacy policy and agree to its terms.

6. Contact

If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us by email at contact@waterinmyspeaker.com.
