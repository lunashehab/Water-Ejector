Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Your privacy is important to us. It is Speaker Cleaner Pro's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our website, https://waterinmyspeaker.com.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal data. Our tool operates entirely client-side (locally on your device). When you play a sound frequency, the audio is generated by your browser. No audio files, microphone data, or personal identifiers are sent to our servers.

2. Cookies & Local Storage

We use "Local Storage" on your device to enhance your experience. This allows us to remember your preferences (such as your selected language or "Day Streak") between visits. This data stays on your device and is never transferred to us.

We also use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Advertising: We use Google AdSense to serve ads. Google may use cookies (such as the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites on the internet.
  • Analytics: We may use anonymous analytics to track basic site usage (e.g., how many people visited today) to improve performance.

3. Advertising (Google AdSense)

Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.

We have no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

4. Links to Other Sites

Our Service may contain links to other sites that are not operated by us (e.g., our partner tool TellMyLocation.com). If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that third party's site. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of every site you visit.

5. Security

Since we do not collect personal information (email, phone numbers, or names), there is no database of user info to be breached. Your usage of this tool is private to your device.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us
