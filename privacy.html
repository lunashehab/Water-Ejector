Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

At Speaker Cleaner Pro ("we," "our," or "us"), we prioritize your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle data when you visit waterinmyspeaker.com.

1. No Data Collection

Speaker Cleaner Pro is a client-side web utility. The audio frequencies are generated directly in your browser using the Web Audio API. We do not record audio, access your microphone, or store any personal information on our servers.

2. Google AdSense & Consent Mode v2

We use Google AdSense to serve advertisements. To comply with privacy regulations (GDPR/CCPA), we have implemented Google Consent Mode v2.

  • Personalized Ads: Only served if you provide explicit consent via our certified Consent Management Platform (CMP).
  • Non-Personalized Ads: If consent is denied, Google receives only aggregate, non-identifying signals to allow basic ad serving without using personal data for profiling.

3. Analytics

We use Simple Analytics for website traffic monitoring. This is a privacy-first tool that does not use cookies, does not collect personal data, and does not track users across websites. Your IP address is never stored.

4. Cookies

Aside from cookies used by Google AdSense (subject to your consent), this website does not use first-party tracking cookies.

5. Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external sites.

6. Contact

If you have questions regarding this policy, contact us at support@waterinmyspeaker.com.
