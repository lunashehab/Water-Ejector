1. The "Zero-Knowledge" Audio Architecture

Unlike other utility applications, our core technology (the "Frequency Generator") operates entirely on the client-side (your local device) via the Web Audio API. This is a critical distinction for your privacy:

  • No Audio Uploads: The sounds you hear are mathematically generated by your browser's CPU in real-time. No audio files are downloaded to or uploaded from your device.
  • No Microphone Access: Our "Speaker Test" and cleaning modes do strictly output-only operations. We do not request, require, or utilize microphone permissions. We cannot listen to you, record you, or analyze your environment.
  • Local Execution: The JavaScript code runs within your browser's sandbox. The "cleaning cycle" does not send telemetry data about your specific hardware health to our servers.
