Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 13, 2026

At Don Systems ("we", "us"), your privacy is our priority. This document outlines how Speaker Cleaner Pro handles data.

1. No Personal Audio Collection

This tool runs locally in your browser. We DO NOT record your microphone, store your files, or transmit audio data to any server. The sound frequencies are generated client-side using the Web Audio API.

2. Cookies & Advertising

We use Google AdSense to serve advertisements. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to your sites and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

3. Analytics

We use Simple Analytics to monitor traffic trends (page views, device types). This service is privacy-first and does not track your IP address or use intrusive fingerprinting.
